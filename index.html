PIRANHA MADNESS!

You are a wandering fish in a body of water, with no direction but to follow your insticnt. Thats right, you guessed it; eat all the fish you can see without getting eaten by the lurking piranhas (those blood thirsty mothers)

You're fish will not like getting hit by a piranha, so much so it will cause it to be in a spin... literaly

Use the arrow buttons to move around the screen to eat up all the fish without dying. Once you have eaten them all, you will progress to the next level. If you stop moving, you will be asleep, which will make the piranhas think you're dead and you will be too limp to eat aby fish.

Level one you will be in a river, then following that the sea, and finally, the ocean where you will be set free!

You will have health points, current level and number of fish eaten at the top of your screen
